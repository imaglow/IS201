Experiential Marketing Campaign

Jan-April 2023
A few of my peers and I developed an experiential marketing event for a local business called The Social--an up and coming hangout spot for college kids. My team dove deep into the target audience and identified that a party welcoming summer would attract the most college students to help increase awareness of The Social. We planned each part of the event, including the social media to advertise, and made a highly visual presentation using only Adobe products (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop). We ended up pitching to the owners of The Social.
